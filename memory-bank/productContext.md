# Product Context

_This document explains why this project exists, the problems it solves, how it should work, and the user experience goals._

## Problem Statement

Potential clients interested in professional sports training need a clear, engaging way to discover FUGA's multidisciplinary training services. They want to understand the available programs, pricing, and registration process before committing. The current scheduling-focused interface doesn't effectively showcase the team's services or drive new client acquisition.

## Target Audience

- **Potential Clients:** Primary users who are interested in strength training and sport-specific programs (cycling, running, swimming).
- **Athletes:** Individuals looking for professional coaching and customized training programs.
- **Fitness Enthusiasts:** People seeking structured strength and conditioning programs.

## User Stories

- As a potential client, I want to quickly understand what services FUGA offers so I can determine if they meet my needs.
- As a potential client, I want to see clear pricing information so I can evaluate the investment.
- As a potential client, I want an easy way to register or request more information.
- As a potential client, I want to learn about the team's expertise and training philosophy.
- As a returning user, I want quick access to the login page to access my account.

## User Experience Goals

- **Immediate Value Proposition:** Visitors should immediately understand FUGA's core services and value.
- **Clear Navigation:** Easy access to all sections (services, pricing, about, contact).
- **Compelling Visual Design:** Strong brand identity with black/white color scheme and athletic aesthetic.
- **Conversion-Focused:** Clear call-to-actions for registration and information requests.
- **Mobile-First:** Responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices.
